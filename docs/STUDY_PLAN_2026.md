## 💡 Success Tips

1. **Consistency**: Code daily, even if just 1 problem
2. **Quality over Quantity**: Master patterns, not just solutions
3. **Explain Out Loud**: Practice communicating your thoughts
4. **Edge Cases**: Always consider null, empty, single element
5. **Time Complexity**: Always analyze before coding
6. **Learn from Others**: Read optimal solutions after attempting
7. **Mock Interviews**: Get feedback on communication
8. **Rest**: Take breaks, sleep well before important days
